Amruteshwara Temple

It is scenic spot lapped in the luxurious abundance of the beauty of the nature as if mother earth is all flourishing in her green attire. The place is dotted with coconut and palm trees, flourishing paddy fields and water streams. This is Sri Amrutheshwara Temple located amid scenic surrounding in a small town called Vamanjoor nearly 15 kms away on the Mangalore- Moodabidri route. Nearly 400 years ago, the family belonging to Kshatri kings from Mayappady of Kumbula ruled the area of Anantha Kody of Ulayibettu Village.
