Roman catholic church

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Udupi is a diocese located in Udupi District of Karnataka, India. It was announced by Pope Benedict XVI on 16 July 2012. It comprises the three civil talukas Udupi, Karkala and Kundapura and is a suffraganof the Archdiocese of Bangalore. The diocese contains 47 parishes, and the parish church of "Our Lady of Miracles", also known as Milagres Church, is the Cathedral church of the dioecese which is situated in Kallianpur of Udupi district.
