Ambaragudda

Ambaragudda is a hill, covering 250 hectares (620 acres) located in Western Ghats near Kodachadri in Hosanagara taluk, Shimoga District in the Indian state of Karnataka. It is covered with rainforests. Mining operations have drawn protests.The Karnataka government declared it as a natural heritage site of Western Ghat region in 2009.
